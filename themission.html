The Mission



Every student deserves to look forward to a special night that is the culmination of their hard work and dedication throughout their High School Career.  Education funding is always limited and in many school districts, event ticket sales cannot fund a successful dance, prom, or homecoming.  At CeleBright The Night we want to eliminate the cancellation of these important milestone events due to a student body that cannot afford the high ticket prices.  We work with rental companies nationwide to procure donations of lighting, special effects, staging, tents, tables, and, chairs, and other important event infrastructure for schools in need. Schools can apply by filling out or Grant Application Form. Rental companies wishing to contribute can find more information here.
