Grant Applications


CeleBright The Night provides grants for entertainment equipment for school dances and events nationwide.


Does your school have the need for equipment to make your dream dance a reality? We want to hear from you.

CeleBright The Night provides grants for audio-visual, lighting, staging, and event catering infrastructure. Our focus is on providing equipment for schools with significant financial need. Our goal is to prevent event cancellations due to the inability of a student body to afford tickets which fund traditionally costly events. Donations are granted based on need and equipment availability.
